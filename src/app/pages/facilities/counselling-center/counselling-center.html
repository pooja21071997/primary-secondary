Counselling Facilities

The school has a dedicated Counselling Centre to provide personal and group counselling to children. The Centre serves as a space for students to discuss their academic, social, emotional and behavioural concerns related to adolescence and peer group dynamics. The facility is also used for providing college and career counselling.

Center conducts a variety of psychological assessments to support the growth and well-being of students, including:

1. Intelligence Test (IQ Test): Designed to measure the cognitive abilities of students.

2. Psychoprofile and Personality Test: Administered primarily to students in 7th and 8th grades, these tests help in understanding individual personality traits and behavioral tendencies.

3. Career Test: Specifically for 10th-grade students, this test aids in identifying suitable career paths based on their interests and aptitudes.

4. Study Habit Test (Optional): This test evaluates students' study patterns and techniques, offering insights for improvement.

5. Emotional Intelligence (EQ) Assessment: This evaluates a student's ability to understand and manage emotions effectively.

6. 16 Personality Factors (16 PF) Test: This provides a comprehensive analysis of a student's personality.

In addition to these tests, the center also addresses various challenges faced by students, parents, and teachers, such as personal issues, behavioral problems, academic struggles, lack of confidence, restlessness, and feelings of inferiority. Experienced psychologists provide counseling and guidance to tackle these issues. Importantly, this facility is inclusive and available to individuals of all ages and educational backgrounds. There are no restrictions based on age or education level for seeking guidance. The center operates within the school premises every Tuesday and Friday, offering convenient and accessible support for students.
