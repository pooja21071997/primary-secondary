Multipurpose Hall

The Multipurpose Hall is a dynamic and versatile space that serves as the heart of academic, cultural, and community engagement within the institution.

01

Guest Lectures & Panel Discussions: Equipped with stage, podium, advanced audio-visual systems and flexible seating.

02

Debates & Elocutions: Excellent acoustics and structured seating ensure clarity and impactful communication.

03

Quizzes & Recitations: Team tables, buzzers, scoreboards and spotlight-ready stage setup.

04

Workshops & Presentations: Flexible arrangements for group discussions and interactive sessions.

05

Meetings & Orientation: Adaptable layout for formal board meetings or welcoming orientation programmes.

06

Exhibitions & Cultural Events: Ideal for performances, screenings, exhibitions and community gatherings.
