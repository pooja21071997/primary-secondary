Sports Facilities

The school strongly believes in the importance of sports for students'[] overall development. Sports help maintain physical fitness, boost mental agility, and build emotional strength. To support this, the school offers excellent facilities, including a large football ground, two volleyball courts, two kho-kho grounds, a cricket ground, and a multipurpose basketball court.

These spaces help students develop teamwork, leadership, discipline, and sportsmanship. Regular participation in sports also improves focus and academic performance. Through these activities, the school nurtures well-rounded individuals who are physically active, mentally sharp, and emotionally balanced.
