Vivekanand Pratishthan Sanchalit Primary & Higher Primary School

Vivekanand Pratishthan Sanchalit Primary & Higher Primary School, established in June 2016, serves as a beacon of educational excellence from Nursery to 10th standard. It stands as a testament to the visionary initiative of the Vivekanand Pratishthan Foundation, dedicated to shaping young minds through an amalgamation of traditional Indian knowledge systems and modern technological advancements. Our primary aim is to democratize education, providing every child with equitable access to learning opportunities regardless of their background.

At the heart of our educational philosophy lies the belief in value-added education. We don't just impart academic knowledge; we foster holistic development by nurturing creativity, compassion, and innovation in our students. Through a dynamic curriculum that integrates both theoretical understanding and practical application, we aim to instill in our students a deep appreciation for their cultural heritage while equipping them with the skills needed to thrive in the digital age.

Central to our mission is the active involvement of parents in their child’s educational journey. We recognize the crucial role parents play as partners in education, and thus, we ensure they are provided with the necessary tools and resources to remain engaged and supportive. Through regular communication channels, parent-teacher collaborations, and workshops, we create a cohesive community where everyone is invested in the success and well-being of our students. Together, we strive to create a nurturing environment where every child can reach their full potential and become responsible global citizens.

Education is meant to make life better, help personal growth, enhance thinking abilities, and lead to good morals.

-Dr. Avinash Ramachandra Acharya

Subsections of Institute

Pre Primary School

Nurtures young children aged 3-5, focusing on early learning, social skills, and creative exploration in a playful environment.

Primary School

Children aged 6-11, offering basic education in literacy, numeracy, and foundational academic and social skills.

Secondary School

An educational stage following primary school, focusing on advanced learning for Class 5 to 10

Principal's Message

          Vivekanand Pratishthan is a sacred center in the field of education. We extend a heartfelt welcome to all parents. The foundation of a child's physical, mental, and social development is laid during early childhood, that is, from birth to six years of age. During this period, 70% of mental development and 40% of physical development takes place. How a child will lead life in the future largely depends on the family environment. Therefore the atmosphere at home should be supportive and nurturing for a child's proper development.
                    In short, if the foundation of a child's development during early childhood is strong, a balanced personality will be formed. Hence, do not rush to make children grow up by taking away their childhood. Avoid saying that you do not have time for your own child. Instead, provide your child with nutritious food, love, care, protection and quality time, and have faith in them - everything will fall into place naturally.

Warm regards,
Hemraj Aadhar Patil
Principal
Vivekanand Pratishthan’s Primary and Higher Primary School
